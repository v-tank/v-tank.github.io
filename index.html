About Me

I’m a 24-year-young guy who still dreams like a 12 year-young kid.

And although my childhood aspiration of being an astronaut did not come true, my journey so far has given me the opportunity to work on some very interesting projects.

I am a recent Engineering graduate from San Jose State University. I enjoy being surrounded by technology and staying at the forefront of innovation, which is why I love the Silicon Valley. Robots fascinated me when I was younger, so I went on to focus my degree in that field. I worked on cool projects, developed tons of skills, and grew my network all the while working on my passions of web and app development, product management, and photography. I strongly believe that there is no end to improvement, which is why I constantly strive to self-teach new skills through online courses and by talking to friends.

Making an impact on the world is important to me, which is why I decided to join the Tech force in the Silicon Valley in December 2017. I want to develop products that millions of people use and love. Currently, I’m enrolled in a coding bootcamp at UC Berkeley to help me sharpen my skills and jump into the Tech world.

That’s just a little about me. Let’s talk so we can learn more about each other.
